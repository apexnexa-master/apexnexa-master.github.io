Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

At PixelDoc, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and process your data when you use the PixelDoc mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

PixelDoc is designed to operate primarily offline. All documents you scan, edit, compile, or sign remain stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your scanned documents to external servers.

2. Permissions Required

To provide core application features, the app may request:

  • Camera: To capture photos of physical documents for scanning.
  • Storage/Media Access: To import existing images for PDF compilation and to save the compiled PDFs to your device's storage.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in the application. AdMob may collect and process unique advertising identifiers and other device metrics to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

4. Security

We use industry-standard precautions to safeguard any data stored on your device. However, since the app runs locally on your operating system, we encourage you to maintain device security to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this policy, please contact us at support@apexnexa.com.
