Privacy Policy

Daily Quiz - GK & Trivia • Last Updated: July 10, 2026

At NexaSoft Studios, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and process your data when you use the Daily Quiz - GK & Trivia mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Daily Quiz is designed as an engaging trivia app. We do not store or collect personal identifying data on remote servers. All your score progress, trivia history, category selections, and learning streaks are processed and stored locally on your mobile device.

2. Device Permissions Required

To provide core functionalities, Daily Quiz may request:

  • Network Connections / Internet Access: Required to fetch updated trivia questions, download daily challenge content, and load advertisements.

3. Advertising and Analytics

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in the application. AdMob may collect and process unique advertising identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) and device metadata to display relevant advertisements. This data collection is handled in compliance with Google Play's developer policies.

4. Data Security

Since your learning progress and game scores are stored locally, we cannot recover them if the application is deleted or if the device is reset. We recommend keeping your device OS backed up.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at support.nexasoft@gmail.com.
